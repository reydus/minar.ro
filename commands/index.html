Factions and protection commands


To begin with, if you walk about, you are likely to stumble across somebody's faction land. This is protected and you may not destroy blocks or open property in the land. You will know whose faction it is by typing:


/f whos <faction name> -- Will give you information about the faction, its members, its lands and its power.


/f invite <user name> -- Invite somebody to join your faction, they will obtain permission in your land and form part of your faction.


/f claim ... - See more parameters within the game. Claims land to your faction.


/f player <player name> -- See player statistics, power, age, etc. Check your power regularly to know you are not in the negative and putting in danger the lands of your faction.


/f join <faction name> -- To join a faction whose leader has invited you.


/f warp ... -- This command servers several purposes-- Use "go" to see a list of the warp-points you can teleport to, and use "set" to describe the warp point you want to create and make accesible for your faction members.


/f money ... ... -- This command is used to manage your faction's total money. This can be used to buy lands. Add money by using deposit <amount> and withdraw <amount>


POWER AND LANDS: In order to get land, you need two things: Power and money. The power of your faction is the sum of the total power of the members. Each member has a maximum power of 10, and will decrease the more they die. They can also, after dying enough times, acquire negative power that will reduce your faction's total power. You can hold as many lands as power you have. For example, if you have two members, each one giving 10 power to the faction (that is 20 power in total), if one of them dies and their power drops down to 8, the total power will be 18. If you own 20 chunks of land, that means somebody could come and conquer two chunks of your lands (and valuables!) for their faction. Be careful, and whatch your power. NOTE: Power may be restored slowly if you or your members don't die for a while.


MONEY: When claiming lands, you will need to pay $60 of your faction currency to own it. It is a single payment. You can add money to your faction's account by using the /f money deposit command listed above.

Server commands


During your stay in the reyd.us minecraft server, you can use a number of commands to get you by. This list can always be expanded as long as it is not too much cheating, just hit me up. ;)


/balance - Print your balance


/help - See commands you can use


/sethome - Set your current position as your home, you can teleport back here by using:


/home - Teleport to the position you set as /sethome.


/list - Print users online, you can also use the TAB key!


/me - Roleplay in third person!


/money - Same as /balance, print your balance.


/motd - View Message of the Day.


/msg <user> - Send a private message to somebody!


/nick <new nick or "off"> - Set your nick, the character "~" will be added to your nickname to indicate it is a nickname. Your realname is still discoverable by using /realname.


/realname - Find the realname of a user that might be using a nickname (That is, when their name is preffixed with "~")


/sell <hand> or <inventory> or <item id> - Sells items for profit. Only ores may be sold for profit.


/warp spawn - will take you to the spawn, the place where players first appear.


/tpa <user> - Ask a user if you could teleport to where they are. This will have a cooldown and may be cancelled if you are attacked, so find a safe place to teleport from!


/tpahere <user> - Ask a user if they could teleport to where you are. Their teleportation may be cancelled if they are attacked as well.


/tpaccept - Accept a teleport petition made by /tpa or /tpahere. Teleportation cooldown will begin when this command is sent.


/sell hand <number> - While holding Diamond ($40), golden ingots ($30) or iron ingots ($10), use this command to sell them for money you can give to your faction. BEWARE: If you do not input a number of items to sell, the command will sell all the items you currently have at hand. So if you intend to sell one (1) diamond, then type /sell hand 1 with the diamond in your hand.

/afk -- Announce that you are busy or Away-from-Keyboard

/suicide -- Commit suicide.
